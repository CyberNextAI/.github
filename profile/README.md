# CyberNextAI Infrastructure

## 中文

### 我们的目标

我们的目标是让 AI **更可靠、更可用、更具生产力**。

我们关注的不是“让模型参数更大”或“把单点指标再提高一点”，而是构建 AI 走向真实生产环境所必需的基础设施。

### 我们不做什么

- 不以优化大模型本身为核心目标。
- 不把主要精力放在训练更大的模型或追逐短期 benchmark 分数。

### 我们在做什么

我们专注于模型基础设施建设，重点包括：

- **Memory**：为 AI 构建可管理、可追溯、可持续演化的记忆能力。
- **Harness**：构建评测与运行框架，让能力验证、质量保障和回归测试可规模化执行。
- **Loop Flow**：设计稳定的任务闭环流程，使 AI 在“理解、执行、反馈、修正”中持续提升交付质量。

### 我们相信

AI 的长期价值，来自稳定可靠的系统工程，而不是一次性的模型奇迹。

## English

### Our Mission

Our mission is to make AI **more reliable, more usable, and more productive**.

We are not focused on making models bigger or squeezing marginal gains from isolated benchmarks. We focus on building the infrastructure that enables AI to work in real production environments.

### What We Do Not Optimize For

- We do not treat large-model optimization as the core objective.
- We do not prioritize bigger training runs or short-term benchmark chasing.

### What We Build

We build foundational infrastructure around models, including:

- **Memory**: Structured, traceable, and evolvable memory systems for AI.
- **Harness**: Evaluation and execution harnesses for scalable validation, quality control, and regression safety.
- **Loop Flow**: Reliable closed-loop workflows that improve outcomes through iterative understand-execute-feedback-correct cycles.

### Our Belief

Long-term AI value comes from dependable systems engineering, not one-off model breakthroughs.
